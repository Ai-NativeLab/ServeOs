RTL / Arabic Adaptations
Mirroring the directional assets
The ServeOS wordmark stays LTR always — it's a proper name, never mirrored, and keeps its coral "OS" on the right of the mark regardless of interface direction. Everything else that implies a reading direction — hero copy blocks, CTA arrows, the login illustration/form split, OG layout — flips for Arabic. Purely symmetric icons (search, add, bell, status dots) need no change at all.
